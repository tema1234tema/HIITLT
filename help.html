Help center

How to Create a Task?

By using our platform, you agree to comply with our service rules. We provide a convenient and secure way for clients and freelancers to interact. Users must provide accurate information, follow community guidelines, and treat each other with respect. The administration reserves the right to modify the terms of use at any time.

How to Find Tasks?

Use the search bar or browse tasks by category. If you find a relevant task, contact the task creator directly through the platform.

How to Promote My Task?

To increase visibility, you can purchase task promotion options:

  • Top placement – your task appears at the top of the list.
  • Highlighting – makes your task stand out.
  • Urgent tag – marks your task as high priority.

How to Contact the Task Creator?

Use the "Message" button on the task page to discuss details and negotiate terms.

How to Delete or Edit My Task?

Go to "My Tasks", select the task, and choose "Edit" or "Delete" from the menu.

Safety Tips

  • Do not share personal payment details.
  • Always meet in safe locations if necessary.
  • Avoid suspicious offers and always verify users.
  • Report any suspicious activity.

Contact Support

For any questions, reach out via support@yourwebsite.com or use the "Help" section.
